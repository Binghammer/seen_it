Privacy Policy for SeenIt

Effective Date: March 23, 2026

SeenIt (“we”, “our”, or “us”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the SeenIt mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

SeenIt is designed to minimize data collection.

a. User-Provided Data

  • Titles you mark as watched
  • Ratings you provide
  • Watch history entries
  • Optional notes or comments

b. Automatically Collected Data

  • Basic app usage data for performance and debugging, if such tooling is enabled
  • Device information such as OS version and app version, if required for crash reporting or diagnostics

2. How We Use Information

We use data to:

  • Display your watch history and app content
  • Improve app functionality
  • Fix bugs and crashes

We do not sell your personal data.

3. Data Storage

  • Your data may be stored locally on your device
  • If cloud features are added later, this Privacy Policy will be updated

4. Third-Party Services

SeenIt may use third-party services such as:

  • TMDB (The Movie Database) for movie and TV metadata
  • Analytics or crash reporting tools, if implemented

These third-party services may collect limited data according to their own privacy policies.

5. Data Sharing

We do not share your personal data with third parties except:

  • When required by law
  • When necessary to provide essential app functionality, such as API requests

6. Your Choices

You can:

  • Delete your data by uninstalling the app
  • Clear app data through your device settings

7. Children's Privacy

SeenIt is not directed to children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with a new effective date.
